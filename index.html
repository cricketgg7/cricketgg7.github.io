Greg Gonzalez is a current student at the University of Florida. There, he's studying media and storytelling through the Digital Worlds Institute and Bachelor of Arts in Digital Arts and Sciences program (BADAS Program), and Japanese language through the East Asian Languages program.


Greg has worked on a multitude of creative projects. His skills include 8 years of practicing photography, 7 years of filming, game design, creative writing, interactive storytelling, and most recently, animation. He's also gotten into the habit of streaming games on Twitch to help raise money for the Extra-Life charity, as well as the social charity group, the Black Death Crew. Greg hopes to open his own production studio to produce films, video games, music, and possibly publish graphic novels.


Why Cricket Media? Short answer: Greg enjoys anime, and enjoyed the Dragon Ball series in his younger days. One of the characters shared his name, and that character just so happened to be a cricket. Since then, Greg has used the screenname Cricketgg7 on particularly every website he's signed up.
